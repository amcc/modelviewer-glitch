Modelviewer / Glitch

How to get a model online for free:

  • Open a free Glitch account here: glitch.com.
  • Start a new project, then click on Assets in the sidebar.
  • Drag your glb / gltf file in to the assets.
  • Click on the uploaded file and Copy URL.
  • Paste the URL in the Media Item field.

Glitch isn't the only way to host your file for free - but its a very good one.
